# ApplyPilot AI - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 16, 2026

ApplyPilot AI ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the ApplyPilot AI Chrome extension and web backend (the "Service").

## Data Collection

### Resume Data
We collect and store resume data you upload, including:
- Full name, email, phone
- Education, experience, skills
- Raw and normalized text representations

Resume data is stored securely in encrypted PostgreSQL databases.

### Job Application Data
We track:
- Company names, job titles, URLs
- Application status, dates, notes
- ATS scores and fit assessments

### User Account Data
- Email address and password (hashed)
- IP address for session management

## Data Usage

We use your data to:
1. Generate ATS scores and resume optimization recommendations
2. Track your application pipeline
3. Provide career insights and skill gap analysis
4. Improve our AI models (with opt-in consent)

We do **NOT**:
- Sell personal data to third parties
- Use data for targeted advertising
- Share data with external AI services (OpenAI, etc.)
- Perform hidden tracking

## Local AI Mode

When you enable Local AI Mode and connect an Ollama endpoint:
- Resume text **never leaves your device**
- All processing happens locally
- We do not access or store locally-processed data

## Data Retention

- Resume data: retained until you delete it
- Application data: retained until you delete it
- Session logs: deleted after 30 days
- Error logs: deleted after 90 days

## Data Security

- **Transport:** HTTPS/TLS for all communication
- **Storage:** AES-256 encryption for sensitive fields
- **Access:** Role-based access control
- **Audit:** All access logged for compliance

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Opt out of non-essential processing
- Receive a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@applypilot.ai

## Third-Party Services

We may use:
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (optional, data anonymized)
- **Supabase Storage:** Resume exports (encrypted)
- **Google OAuth:** Authentication (optional)

Third parties are bound by confidentiality agreements.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13. If we learn we have, we will delete it immediately.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions, email: privacy@applypilot.ai

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify users of material changes via email.
